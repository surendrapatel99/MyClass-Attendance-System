The Khallikote Autonomous College introduced 3 years Master of Computer Applications (MCA) under self-financing mode in 1997. The MCA course has been approved by AICTE and affiliated to Berhampur University. MCA course has been designed to generate quality System Analyst and software professionals.

Now the Khallikote Autonomous College is upgraded to Khallikote Unitary University from 1st August 2021.


ADMISSION PROCEDURE FOR MCA COURSE

Students are taking admission into the MCA course through Odisha JEE conducted by BPUT. So the College ensure quality intake for its courses.

COURSES OFFERED
Khallikote Autonomous College provided 3 years MCA course.
MCA Eligibility from the session 2020
From 2020 session the MCA course is 2 years duration.
Eligibility:

All those candidates who have passed bachelor’s degree of minimum three years duration in BCA/B.Sc.(IT)/B.Sc.(CS) or equivalent/B.Voc. with Computer as a major subject and with mathematics at 10+2 level or at graduation level.

or

Bachelor Degree in Computer Science & Engineering or equivalent.


or

Any bachelor’s degree of minimum three years duration with mathematics at 10+2 level or at graduation level and minimum One Year Diploma in Computer Application/Science/IT or equivalent from any recognized University/Institution.
